Hearing Aids

A hearing aid is a battery-powered electronic device designed to improve your hearing. Small enough to wear in or behind your ear, they make some sounds louder.

Audiometry

A pure tone audiometry test measures the softest, or least audible, sound that a person can hear. Pure tone audiometry charts the hearing level of different tone frequencies in both ears

Rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty is surgery that changes the shape of the nose. The motivation for rhinoplasty may be to change the appearance of the nose, improve breathing or both.

Learn more..
Snoring Surgery

Snoring Surgery (Uvulopalatopharyngoplasty) is a surgical procedure used to remove redundant soft tissue in the back of the throat to prevent the airway obstruction occurring during sleep.

Learn more..
Cataract

A cataract is an opacification of the lens of the eye which leads to a decrease in vision. Cataract surgery is a procedure to remove the lens of your eye and, in most cases, replace it with an artificial lens.

Learn more..
Glaucoma

Glaucoma is one of the leading causes of blindness for people over the age of 60. It is caused by the damage the optic nerve, the health of which is vital for good vision.
