Dr Sanjay Gala

  • Experience: 25+years
  • Speciality: ENT
  • Education: MS Surgery, Grant Medical College

Biography

Dr Sanjay Gala completed his MS (ENT) in the year 1991 and is a private practitioner since the last 25+ years. His personal clinic in Nana Chowk, Grant Road is a complete ENT setup with most facilities in ENT Diagnostics and Surgery. He brings in all his experience and innovations in treating patients. He also heads semi charitable setup, Maru Hospital in Parel for more than 15 years. He is attached to a number of tertiary care hospitals in South Mumbai including Bhatia Hospital, Wokhardt Hospital and so on.
