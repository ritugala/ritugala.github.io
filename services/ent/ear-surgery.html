Ear Surgery

When it comes to ear surgery, no matter how routine or complex the procedure, you want the very best outcome and an experienced surgeon who performs ear surgery routinely. At Dr Gala Nursing Home we have a combined experience of more than 28 years performing ear surgery exclusively. Since we don’t do anything else, operations that may seem major at other practices become a matter of routine for us.

Our comprehensive evaluation includes a thorough history, a complete exam and diagnostic testing that is suitable to your condition. This may include audiologic testing with audiogram and tympanogram, vestibular testing, a CAT scan or MRI, or even advanced studies such as electrocochleography, vestibular evoked myogenic potentials, or electrodiagnostic testing to assess the function of the facial nerve

We offer the complete scope of surgical treatments. For rehabilitation of hearing loss, we perform stapedectomy for otosclerosis, middle ear implants, cochlear implants,and bone conduction implants such as the BAHA and Ponto devices. For chronic ear infections, we offer tympanoplasty and mastoidectomy and osscicular reconstruction.
